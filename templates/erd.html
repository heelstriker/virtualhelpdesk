Entity Relationship Diagram

VirtualHelpDesk is built on a SQLite database consisting of 11 tables divided into two layers. Transaction tables hold live operational data generated as devices are managed, patched, and monitored across Banana Corporation. Catalog tables act as master data — they define what software, patches, and printers exist in the environment and serve as the reference source that transaction records are validated against. All transaction tables are linked through a shared hostname key, keeping every endpoint's data traceable back to a single device identity.
