VirtualHelpDesk

Enterprise IT Operations & Endpoint Management Simulation Platform

🧭 About This Project

VirtualHelpDesk is built on top of 17 years of hands-on IT operations experience — not just years spent in on-premises environments, but years spent keeping mission-critical systems running for organizations that couldn't afford downtime: patch windows with hard deadlines, endpoint fleets under audit, and incidents where the fix had to work the first time.

This isn't a tutorial exercise. It's an attempt to reconstruct, page by page, the kind of environment that experience lives in — a live operations dashboard, a real CMDB-style data model, the PowerShell commands actually used in the field, and the project coordination that a PC refresh or infrastructure change requires beyond just running a script.

As the project has grown past a single dashboard, this page now works as a map — a short summary of what each part covers and why it's here.
